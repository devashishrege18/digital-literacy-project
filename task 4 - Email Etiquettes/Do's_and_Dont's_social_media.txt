Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist

Do's:

1. Think carefully before posting anything online.
2. Use respectful language while interacting with others.
3. Verify information before sharing it.
4. Keep personal information private.
5. Maintain a professional online image.

Don'ts:

1. Do not share fake news or unverified content.
2. Do not post offensive comments or messages.
3. Do not share passwords with anyone.
4. Do not engage in online arguments unnecessarily.
5. Do not upload content that may harm your reputation.
