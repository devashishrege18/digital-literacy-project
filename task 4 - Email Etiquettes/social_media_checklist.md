# Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist

## Do's

* Think carefully before posting anything online.
* Use respectful language while interacting with others.
* Verify information before sharing it.
* Keep personal information private.
* Maintain a professional online image.

## Don'ts

* Do not share fake news or unverified content.
* Do not post offensive comments or messages.
* Do not share passwords with anyone.
* Do not engage in unnecessary online arguments.
* Do not upload content that may harm your reputation.
