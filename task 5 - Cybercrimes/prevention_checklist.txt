Stay Safe Online – UPI Fraud Prevention Checklist

1. Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone.
2. Remember that receiving money never requires entering your UPI PIN.
3. Verify every payment request before approving it.
4. Do not scan unknown QR codes sent by strangers.
5. Avoid clicking suspicious payment links received through SMS or WhatsApp.
6. Use only official banking and payment applications.
7. Check customer care numbers only from official websites.
8. Enable transaction alerts on your bank account.
9. Lock payment apps with fingerprint or password security.
10. If fraud happens, immediately report it at cybercrime.gov.in.
11. Call national cybercrime helpline 1930 without delay.
12. Regularly review bank transaction history for suspicious activity.
